Market Recap & Vision

From the recovery of 2025 to the smart evolution of 2026.

2025 Recap

2025 was the year of "The Great Return." Pattaya saw a 35% surge in international arrivals. The market shifted from rental yield focus to luxury holiday homes, driven by strong demand from Eastern Europe.

2026 Outlook

Wellness & Sustainability. Buyers will prioritize Green Buildings and Health-Tech. Prices in Jomtien are projected to rise by 5-7% as land scarcity becomes a reality.

EEC Progress

The High-Speed Rail linking 3 airports has entered the "Track Laying Phase." U-Tapao Airport's new terminal foundation is 80% complete, aiming for 2027 tests.
