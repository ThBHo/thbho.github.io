High Human Development

การพัฒนาทรัพยากรมนุษย์ระดับสูง

According to the 2025 UNDP Framework, Thailand maintains a highly robust position globally.

  • Ranked 76th Globally: Officially in the "High Human Development" tier.
  • Resilient Healthcare: Driven by strong universal health coverage and rising life expectancy (79.7 years).
  • Gender Empowerment: A rare achievement where Female HDI outpaces Male HDI.
  • Future Education: Expected years of schooling is highly competitive at nearly 16 years.

Summary: Thailand is in the 'High Human Development' group, excelling in universal healthcare, rising life expectancy, educational opportunities, and notable gender equality.

World's Lowest Misery Index

ดัชนีความทุกข์ยากต่ำที่สุดในโลก

Thailand offers exceptional economic stability, ranking as the least miserable country globally.

  • Score of 5.00: The absolute lowest (best) score globally out of over 150 countries.
  • Economic Stability: Reflects manageable inflation and low unemployment.

Summary: Thailand has the lowest misery index in the world (5.00), reflecting a stable economy and low unemployment.

#1 in ASEAN for Green Transition

อันดับ 1 ของอาเซียนด้านการเปลี่ยนผ่านสีเขียว

Thailand leads the region in balancing rapid urban development with environmental sustainability.

  • Top PHDI Score: Ranks #1 in ASEAN for the Planetary-Pressures-Adjusted HDI (0.726).
  • Nature-Positive Growth: Successfully decoupling economic growth from environmental degradation.
  • BCG Economy: Championing the Bio-Circular-Green model for highly livable smart cities.

Summary: Thailand is #1 in ASEAN for environmentally conscious development (PHDI).

Ready for the Global AI Age

พร้อมก้าวสู่อนาคตในยุค AI

Thailand is forward-thinking, actively integrating advanced technology to enhance the quality of life for its citizens.

  • Complementarity Economy: Building systems where AI augments and supports human capabilities.
  • Education Sandbox: Creating new fast-track university models to produce "work-ready" tech talents.
  • Smart Integration: Expanding "AI for All" literacy and adopting AI in public healthcare.

Summary: Thailand focuses on human-AI collaboration, tech education sandboxes, and making AI accessible for all.
