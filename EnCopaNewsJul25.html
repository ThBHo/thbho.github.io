Construction Update: July 2025

Continuously Reaching for the Jomtien Sky!

Greetings to all co-owners and valued customers.

As we officially enter July, we are pleased to present the latest progress of the Copacabana Coral Reef Jomtien project, reflecting the commitment and dedication of our quality construction team, Porn Pranakorn Company Limited, who are working tirelessly to bring this world-class residential project to life.

From the latest photos taken on July 1, 2025, we have significant progress to report:

  • Remarkable Increase in Building Height: The main structure of the residential building has impressively risen. Currently, the structural work has reached the 15th floor, a clear growth from the previous month, reinforcing that our project is soaring towards the Jomtien skyline gracefully as planned.
  • Overview of On-site Activities: Aerial views show construction activities in full swing. All 8 cranes continue to operate, transporting materials and structural components to the top floors, while engineers and workers are actively engaged in rebar installation and concrete pouring, which are crucial for the building's strength and standards.
  • Quality and Safety are Our Top Priorities: As always, we maintain the highest safety standards in every step of our work. Safety nets are installed around the building on the construction floors to ensure the safety of our team and the surrounding community.

Every day that passes is another step that brings our vision of creating "The Jewels of the Sea" closer to reality. We thank you for your continued trust and support and look forward to welcoming you all to the most perfect new home by the scheduled completion in 2027.

We will continue to provide you with monthly progress updates!

Copacabana Coral Reef Jomtien is not just a residence; it's a new landmark on Jomtien Beach, inspired by "The Jewels of the Sea." This 55-story tower is designed to be a symbol of luxury seamlessly blended with the beauty of the sea.

The Future of Travel

The Purple Line Monorail and Its Impact on Your Investment

A Feeder to the EEC

This project is not just an urban mass transit system; it's designed to act as a capillary network connecting travel from all corners of the city to the Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC) and serves as a crucial feeder system for the High-Speed Rail connecting three airports.

Connecting Every Lifestyle

With a route covering over 11.44 kilometers through 12 stations, from Pattaya's central business district to residential communities and government offices in the Nong Prue area, travel becomes more convenient and seamless.

Increasing Investment Value

The arrival of such a large-scale mass transit system is a key factor that will significantly stimulate the economy and increase the value of real estate along its route. Your investment today is an opportunity for future growth.

Although the project is currently awaiting Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) approval, it demonstrates Pattaya City's strong commitment to upgrading its infrastructure, which will undoubtedly benefit your quality of life and the value of your investment in the long term.
